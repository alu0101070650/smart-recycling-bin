The future for our cities

What is Smart Recycling Bin about?

Our main goal is to improve the recycling experience in cities of the future. We support the idea of smart cities in a way that there are benefits for the environment and the inhabitants. In order to actually reach this we develop the idea of a smarter dumpster which motivates the people to recycle through the use of gamification.

The promised experience:

You have some rubbish in your house and no motivation to bring it out? Just imagine it would be fun to do it. You go out, throw the rubbish bag into the friendly looking dumpster and even get a reward for doing it, because you contribute to bring your district to the first rank. After throwing your trash away the display shows you information about the amount of saved CO2 because of your decision to separate and to support recycling. You get information about how much your district is recycling as well and try to become the most environmentally friendly district of the city.

What we stand for

Smart Cities

We connect. It's not only the better recycling experience. Furthermore the dumpsters are connected to our server and with the help of their height sensors it will be possible to collect the garbage in a smarter way.

Recycling

We make the world a better place. We will recycle with more pleasure and the Smart Recycling Bins even can inform and educate you about the seperation of your garbage.

Gamification

In our Smart Recycling Bins we applicate game-design elements and game principles in order to improve the user engagement. You get a reward for recycling and can see on a scoreboard how much your quater is contributing to the cities recycling.
